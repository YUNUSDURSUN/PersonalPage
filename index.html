Biography

I am an Assistant Professor at Marmara University working in wireless communications and optimization for next-generation networks. My research focuses on NOMA-based transmission, MIMO systems, mobile edge computing, physical layer security, and optimization methods for 6G communication systems.

Research Interests

  • Non-Orthogonal Multiple Access (NOMA)
  • Multiple-Input Multiple-Output (MIMO) systems
  • 6G and beyond wireless networks
  • Mobile Edge Computing (MEC)
  • Physical Layer Security
  • Convex Optimization and resource allocation
