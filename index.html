Biography

I am an academic researcher at Marmara University working in wireless communications, with a particular focus on NOMA, MIMO, 6G networks, and convex optimization. My research interests include advanced signal processing algorithms and resource allocation methods for next-generation communication systems.

Research Interests

  • Non-Orthogonal Multiple Access (NOMA)
  • Multiple-Input Multiple-Output (MIMO) Systems
  • 6G Wireless Networks
  • Convex Optimization
  • Signal Processing for Wireless Communications
